    Project 1

    The Effects of Varying Ecological Landscapes on Salamander Abundance

    My project was a brief study conducted during the summer in 2022 on Salamander abundance. More specifically, I chose to study the impact of varying ecological landscapes on salamander abundance. I was selected to conduct this projec through the ACA where I was selected based on my application which conisted of an in-depth research proposal. Once I had collected all data, I cleaned and analyzed the data inside R. I created many occupancy models during my analysis and created a scatterplot to display my results which can be seen in the paper. My presentation of this project can be found here.
